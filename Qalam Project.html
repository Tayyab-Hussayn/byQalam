◆ Founder Story

I was invisible on LinkedIn.
So I built the tool I needed.

6 years in HR. CPA-HR, CHRMP, TRCP certified. I knew my stuff. But on LinkedIn? I was a ghost. I'd post once a month, get 12 likes, and disappear for 3 weeks.

I tried ChatGPT. It wrote like a brochure. I tried Jasper. It wrote like a marketer. I needed something that wrote like me — direct, HR-specific, occasionally sarcastic, always honest.

So I built Qalam in Lahore, on a PKR 1,000/month budget (~$3.50 USD), while working full-time as People & Culture Manager. Every feature was built because I needed it first. Every price was set because I know what professionals in Pakistan can actually afford.

This isn't Silicon Valley software with Pakistani pricing slapped on. This is built here, for here, by someone who lives here.
